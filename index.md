## About Me

Cheng Xiao is a Postdoctoral Fellow at the [Feilong Lab](https://feilonglab.github.io) in the Department of Psychology at the University of South Carolina. At Feilong Lab, she uses computational methods and neuroimaging data to study individual differences in language processing. Her broader research investigates how language and emotion interact in native speakers and second language learners. Cheng holds a PhD in Linguistics from the University of South Carolina, an MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics, a BA in Humanities, a BEc in Finance from Wuhan University.
